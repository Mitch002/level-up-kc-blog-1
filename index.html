Common Foundation Issues

Cracks in Walls or Floors

Cracks are like the flu of foundation issues—common but problematic. They can be a sign of a shifting foundation, which is a big no-no.

Bowed Walls

Ever seen a wall that looks like it's doing the limbo? That's a bowed wall, and it's a sign of serious pressure issues.

Uneven Foundation

If your home is leaning like the Tower of Pisa, you've got an uneven foundation. It's more than just a quirky feature; it's a red flag.

List of Common Issues

  • Cracks in walls or floors
  • Bowed walls
  • Uneven foundation
